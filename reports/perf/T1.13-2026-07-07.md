## Scope

- Added `forge-core` Criterion bench target `kernel`.
- Covered clone, legal-action enumeration, action application, and deterministic
  four-turn playout.
- Added `tools/criterion_to_perf.py` to export Criterion estimates into
  `metrics/perf_current.json`.

- Updated `scripts/perf_smoke.sh`, `scripts/vl.sh` wiring, and recurring perf CI
  so the perf gate generates current metrics before `tools/perf_diff.py`.

  command.
